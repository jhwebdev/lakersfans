With great talent within these four rookies, they shall develop and become stronger players. Lonzo Ball with his playmaking skills shall push the offense forward and to have a fast paced game. Kyle Kuzma is the all around player you need, he gets points, rebounds, assists, steals, blocks, and not to mention those flashy highlights.
